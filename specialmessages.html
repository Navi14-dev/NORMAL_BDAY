A Letter From Your Brother

My dearest Chinnu,

As I write this letter to you on your special day, I can’t help but think about how life has a beautiful way of bringing the right people into our lives at just the right time. Even though we’re not bound by blood, you're every bit my sister — not by birth, but by bond.

We first crossed paths at GIET Engineering College, and though we were just acquaintances in the first year, something shifted towards the end. By the second year, our bond had grown into something truly special — unshakable, honest, and deeply meaningful. From that point on, it wasn’t just friendship anymore. We became family. One for one, always.

You’ve carved a permanent place in my heart. I’ve seen your warmth, your determination, your fierce loyalty — and I've admired all of it. You may not be my sister by blood, but I’ve always cared for you as if you were. You've made me laugh in my darkest times and stood by me like only a true sibling could.

Thank you for being a part of my life, for letting me be the brother who watches out for you, cheers for you, and sometimes annoys you — because that’s what brothers do best, right?

On your birthday, I just want you to know that having you in my life is a gift I’ll always treasure. I’m proud of the woman you’re becoming, and I’ll always be here — to support you, protect you, and remind you that no matter what, you’re never alone.

May this year bring you happiness in every form, dreams that turn into reality, and moments that become cherished memories.
With all my love,
Your Brother
